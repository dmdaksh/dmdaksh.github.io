I am a Graduate Student Instructor (GSI) for SI 330 - Data Manipulation for the Fall 2023 and Winter 2024 semesters. Teaching has been a very rewarding experience, as it has improved me as both a researcher and a person. The experience of teaching has honed my communication skills.
